ArtiMinds RPS is redefining the way of robot programming. Even complex force-torque adaptive robot motions can be programmed within minutes instead of days or weeks.

What we offer:

Intuitive on- and offline programming

Program robot tasks by drag’n’drop of elementary motions using the RPS wizards. On- or offline.

100x faster programming

Combine our 50+ wizards to solve complex robot tasks without writing a single line of code. Reduce robot programming, setup time and later adaptations by up to a factor of 100.

CAD visualization

Use 3D models for motion planning and optimization (optional). Obstacles – both real objects or virtual zones – are automatically avoided by motion planning.

Force controlled robot motions

Teach complex force-controlled assembly processes within minutes. Examples are: peg-in-hole, contacting, alignment, centering, or safe grasping/releasing.

Robust execution

Generate bug-free script code automatically and optimize cycle-time. Run the program directly on the robot controller – ArtiMinds RPS is not used during robot task execution.

Broad hardware support

Various robots, grippers, F/T sensors and standard I/O hardware are supported, e.g., Universal Robots UR5, Robotiq FT150 or your standard pneumatic gripper. Choose the hardware most suited for your application.
